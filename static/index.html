Add a Voice

Upload 5–15 seconds of clear speech for best results. The voice is cloned using F5-TTS (first use downloads the model).

Best outcome tips
  • Audio quality: Use a good microphone in a quiet room. No background music, TV, or fan noise.
  • Duration: 5–15 seconds is optimal. Too short and the voice lacks character; too long adds processing time with diminishing returns.
  • Content: Read a full sentence naturally, at your normal pace. Avoid whispering or exaggerated speech.
  • Format: WAV at 24 kHz mono is ideal. MP3/M4A/FLAC are accepted and converted automatically.
  • Single speaker only. No overlapping voices or interruptions.
  • Consistent tone: Match the energy you want in the output. A calm reading voice produces calm output.
Reading and writing

Edit shows the raw Markdown for writing. Read renders it as a clean page. Toggle with the buttons at top or press Ctrl+E. Save as a .md file with Ctrl+S.

Read Along

In Edit mode, place the cursor where you want to start; in Read mode, click a sentence to anchor it. Press Play. Lector synthesizes audio one sentence at a time using a local TTS engine. First playback after launch takes ~15 seconds while the model loads; subsequent sentences are immediate. Use Volume in the toolbar to adjust output level.

Keyboard shortcuts (work while typing):
Shift+Space  Play / Pause  ·  Alt+,  Previous sentence  ·  Alt+.  Next sentence  ·  Esc  Stop

When the editor isn't focused (e.g. in Read mode), plain Space also toggles Play / Pause.

Pausing mid-sentence rewinds one sentence on resume so you keep the spoken context for the line you were about to edit. Pausing at a sentence boundary resumes normally.

Marks

Shift-click a sentence in Read mode to mark it, or press Alt+M while listening to mark the sentence currently being read. Open Marks to see the list, jump to one in Edit mode, or remove it. Marks are session-scoped and re-anchor whenever the marked text still exists.

Voices

Twelve built-in Kokoro voices (American and British English, male and female). Open Voices to clone a custom voice from a 5-to-15 second WAV/MP3 sample. Custom voices use F5-TTS and produce one sentence every ~10 seconds on a recent GPU.

Encrypted library

Open Library to save drafts encrypted at rest with a key stored in your OS keychain. Nothing leaves your machine. Export your recovery key before reinstalling.
